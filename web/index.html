🎮 LVS-Gateway Installer

Flash your ESP32 device directly from your browser

📋 What is LVS-Gateway?

ESP32 firmware that translates Lovense BLE protocol to control MuSE, LoveSpouse, and Leten toys.

  • Works with XToys.app and buttplug.io
  • Supports multiple devices simultaneously
  • Graphical interface on CYD displays

🔧 Supported Hardware

ESP32
ESP32-S3
CYD (ESP32)

Ready to install?

Connect your ESP32 device via USB and click the button below

⚠️ Important: This installer requires a Chromium-based browser (Chrome, Edge, Opera). Make sure your ESP32 is connected via USB before clicking install.
